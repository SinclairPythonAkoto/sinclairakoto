Brand

Since their conception in 1966, Vans’ classic deck shoes have become contemporary footwear icons. Founder Paul Van Doren opened his first store in California, and quickly saw Vans adopted by the wider skate community who clamoured for the shoes in more colours. With their thick vulcanised rubber soles and moulded waffle grips, Vans’ slip-on and lace up trainers now come in over 60 styles, alongside a comprehensive line of printed tees, logo sweatshirts and backpacks.
